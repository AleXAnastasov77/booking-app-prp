Facilities

🌊 Lakeside Fun

Enjoy our scenic lake with exciting activities and a relaxing beachside atmosphere!

🏖️ Sandy Beach & Swimming

Relax on our soft sandy beach, enjoy safe swimming areas, and soak up the sun. Lifeguards on duty for peace of mind!

🚣‍♂️ Rowboats & Paddleboats

Take a peaceful ride or challenge your friends to a race! Boats are available for rent daily from our lakeside dock.

🎠 Kids’ Corner – Adventure for the Little Ones!

A world of fun, games, and creativity—just for kids! Whether it’s indoors or out, your little adventurers will be in good hands with our amazing animation team.

🌙 Evening Fun with the Animation Team!

The fun doesn’t stop when the sun sets! Our animation team hosts amazing themed evening events to keep the whole family entertained under the stars.

🎤 Welcome Party & Karaoke Night

Kick off the week with a fun welcome party and sing your heart out with family-friendly karaoke. Special “Kids’ Hour” followed by an adults-only round!

🔥 Campfire & Marshmallow Night

Gather around a cozy bonfire by the lake. Roast marshmallows, enjoy DIY s’mores, and listen to acoustic tunes or fun fairy tales!

🪩 Glow Party & Mini Disco

Get your glow on with neon lights, glow sticks, face painting, and a mini disco! Dance to a mix of kids' tunes and party classics.

🎭 Talent Show & Game Night

Show off your talents in a family-friendly talent show! Compete in games like charades, trivia, and more, with fun prizes for the winners.

🍹 Tropical Beach Party

Feel the Hawaiian vibes with tiki torches, limbo contests, and tropical drinks. Fun games, dance-offs, and fire dancers will light up the night!

🎥 Movie Night Under the Stars

Enjoy an outdoor cinema experience with cozy seating and popcorn. Family-friendly films before 9 PM and classic movies for adults afterward.

🎊 Farewell Party & Live Music

Celebrate the end of a fantastic week with live music, dancing, and a special slideshow of the best moments of the week at Foneyn Holiday Park!

🛍️ On-Site Shops – Everything You Need, Right Here!

No need to leave the park! Grab a snack, pick out a new summer outfit, or stock up on essentials—all without leaving the fun behind!

🥐 The Mini Market

From fresh bread to tasty snacks and baby essentials, our mini market keeps your cabin stocked and your belly happy!

👙 Beach & Fashion Shop

Forgot your swimsuit or want a new holiday look? Swing by for summer clothes, sunglasses, flip-flops, and fun souvenirs!

💊 The Park Pharmacy

For life’s little hiccups! Stocked with first aid, cold meds, mosquito spray, baby products, and more—just in case.

🚗 Easy & Secure Parking

Start your holiday stress-free! Our gated system and designated parking make arrival smooth and simple.

🅿️ Hassle-Free Parking

Every cabin has its own parking spot, plus extra spaces for guests with mobility needs near the reception. Well-lit and monitored, your car is in good hands!

Register your license plate when booking, and our smart gate opens automatically when you arrive—no need to stop or check in manually. Vacation mode: ON!
