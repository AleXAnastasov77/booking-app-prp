🌿 The History of Fonteyn Holiday Park

Fonteyn Holiday Park began its journey in the 1980s, with a vision to create a cozy, family-friendly escape in the heart of the Netherlands. What started as a small lakeside retreat quickly grew into a beloved destination for families, nature lovers, and adventure seekers alike.

Due to its success, Fonteyn expanded 10 years later, opening new parks in Belgium and Germany, each carefully designed to reflect the beauty and charm of its surroundings. Today, the Fonteyn Holiday Parks continue to offer a perfect mix of relaxation, fun, and nature, welcoming guests from all over Europe.
